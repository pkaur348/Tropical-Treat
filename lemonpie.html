LEMON MERINGUE PIE



Lemon Meringue Pie ($5.50)

Lemon Meringue Pie is a mouth-watering delight that is loved by all the foodies with a sweet tooth. Made using all purpose flour, eggs, sugar, lemons, butter and baking powder, this is a luscious dessert recipe that is a perfect blend of sweet-sour flavors. This Continental recipe can be enjoyed on occasions like kitty parties, potlucks, birthdays, game nights and anniversaries.

  1. Prep time - 6 hours
  2. Cooking time - 1 hour 10 min
  3. Skill level - Intermediate
  4. Serves - 1 9-inch pie

INGREDIENTS

Homemade Pie Crust - 5 large egg yolks, 1 and 1/3 cups water, 1 cup granulated sugar, 1/3 cup cornstarch,1/4 teaspoon salt, 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice, 1 Tablespoon lemon zest, 2 Tablespoons unsalted butter, softened to room temperature.
Meringue - 5 large egg whites, 1/2 teaspoon cream of tartar, 1/2 cup granulated sugar, 1/8 teaspoon salt

INSTRUCTIONS

Take a saucepan and add all purpose flour, salt, baking powder, corn starch, water and granulated sugar. Whisk it well and place it over medium flame. Let it come to a boil and squeeze lemons in it. Keep stirring and add lemon zest in it. Next, add the butter in it and boil the mixture. Stir it continuously. Meanwhile, preheat the oven at 190 degrees Celsius. Now, add the egg yolks in a bowl with a cup of the sugar-lemon mix and whisk it well. Once the mixture turns foamy, transfer it in the saucepan with the rest of the sugar-lemon mix. Mix it well and let it come to a boil. Once it thickens, take it off the flame and transfer it in the pie mould containing the baked the pie crust. For the meringue, add the egg whites and powdered sugar in a large bowl. Whisk it swiftly in one direction until it turns fluffy. This mixture must form peaks. Once done, pour this mixture and slather it all over the pie. Transfer this in the preheated oven and bake the pie for 12 minutes. Once the pie is baked, take it out from the oven and slice it. Serve it warm to enjoy!
